Legal Aid and Pro Bono Services

Legal Aid Society -- New York City

legalaidnyc.org

Free legal services for low-income New Yorkers. Civil practice includes bankruptcy assistance.

New York Legal Assistance Group (NYLAG)

nylag.org

Free bankruptcy clinics and full-representation bankruptcy assistance for qualifying individuals.

Brooklyn Bar Association Volunteer Lawyers Project

brooklynbar.org

Pro bono legal assistance including bankruptcy matters for qualifying Brooklyn residents.

Nassau/Suffolk Law Services

nslawservices.org

Free civil legal services for Long Island residents, including bankruptcy assistance.

Free Screening and Research Tools

1328(f) Discharge Eligibility Screener

1328f.com

Free tool to check whether a prior discharge creates a time bar under 11 U.S.C. section 1328(f). Runs entirely in your browser -- no data collected.

Open Bankruptcy Project

openbankruptcyproject.org

National research platform analyzing Chapter 13 outcomes across all 94 federal districts. Open-source data and methodology.

1328(f) Research Platform

1328f.org

In-depth research on repeat filing patterns and 1328(f) eligibility violations in the federal bankruptcy system.

Federal Resources

U.S. Trustee Program

justice.gov/ust

The DOJ component responsible for overseeing the administration of bankruptcy cases. File complaints about attorney misconduct or trustee issues.

PACER -- Public Access to Court Electronic Records

pacer.uscourts.gov

Access federal court records including bankruptcy dockets. Fee-based ($0.10/page, capped at $3.00/document).

Federal Judicial Center -- Integrated Database

fjc.gov/research/idb

The source database for all statistics on this site. Public data on all federal court cases.
